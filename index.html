2. Information We Collect

To provide and improve the App's core functionality, which is to alert you when you approach a pre-set destination, we collect the following types of information:

  • Precise Location Data (GPS): The App requires access to your device's precise location (latitude and longitude) to determine your current position and proximity to your set destination. This location data is used to trigger the geofence alarm. Location access is required both when the app is in use (foreground) and, if you enable it for continuous monitoring, when the app is in the background or your phone is locked.
  • Destination Data: We store the destination(s) you set within the App on your local device. This information is necessary for the App to function and provide alerts.
  • Device Information (Potentially): We may collect non-personally identifiable information about your device, such as device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for troubleshooting, analytics to improve app performance, and to ensure compatibility. This data is typically aggregated and anonymized.
  • Background Location Disclosure (Google Play requirement): Stop Alert continuously monitors your location in the background only to deliver destination alarms. The data is never used for advertising or shared for unrelated purposes, and monitoring stops immediately after your alarm triggers or is cancelled. Battery‑optimised APIs are used to minimise power consumption.

Important Note on Background Location: The core functionality of Stop Alert (providing timely alarms as you approach your destination, even if you are not actively using the app or your phone is locked) requires background location access. You will be prompted to grant this permission. If you do not grant background location permission, the alarm feature may not work reliably when the app is not in the foreground.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the Service: Primarily, to monitor your location relative to your set destination and trigger an alarm and full-screen notification when you enter the defined alert radius (geofence).
  • To Improve the App: We may use aggregated and anonymized data to understand how users interact with the App, identify areas for improvement, and enhance user experience.
  • To Ensure Functionality: To ensure the app operates correctly, including background operation for timely alerts.
  • To Communicate With You (Rarely): We may use your information to respond to your inquiries or support requests if you contact us.
